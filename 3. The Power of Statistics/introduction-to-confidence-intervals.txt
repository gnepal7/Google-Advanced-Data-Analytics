# Introduction to confidence intervals
  + Point estimate
    - Uses a single value to estimate a population parameter
  + Confidence interval
    . Sample statistic
    . Margin of error
      - The maximum expected difference between a population parameter and a sample estimate.
      - Formula, margin of error = z-score * SE
    . Confidence level
      - Describes the liklihood that a particular sampling method will produce a confidence 
        interval that includes the population parameter
      - Common levels are 90%, 95%, and 99%
