# How sampling affects your data
  + Statistic
    - A charecteristic of a sample
  + Parameter
    - A charecteristic of a population
  + Point estimate
    - Uses a single value to estimate a population parameter.
  + Sampling distribution
    - A probability distribution of a sample statistis
  + Samplint variability
    - How much an estimate varies between samples  
  + Standard error
    - Larger standard error = sample means and more spread out
    - Smaller standard error = sample means are closer together
    . As sample size gets larger, standard error gets smaller
