# Methods for handling missing data
  + Missing data are often encoded as:
   - N/A
   - Nan('Not a number')
   - [blank]
  + What to do with missing data:
   - Request the missing values to be filled by owner of the data
   - Delete the missing column(s), row(s), or value(s)
   - Create a NaN category
   - Derive new representative value(s)
    . Forward filling
    . Backward filling (backfilling)
    . Deriving mean values
    . Deriving median values
