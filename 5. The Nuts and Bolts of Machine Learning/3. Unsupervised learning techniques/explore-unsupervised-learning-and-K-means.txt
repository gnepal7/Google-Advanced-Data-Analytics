# Introduction to K-means
  + K-means
    - Centriod
      => The center of cluster determined by the mathematical mean
         of all the points in that cluster.
      => K represents the number of cluster in the model.
    - Steps of K-means:
      1. Initiate k centroids
      2. Assign all points to their nearest centroid 
      3. Recalculate the centroid of each cluster based on the 
         point assigned to it.
      4. Repeat 2/3.
