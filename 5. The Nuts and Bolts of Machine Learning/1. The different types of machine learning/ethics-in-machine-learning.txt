# Equity and fairness in machine learning
  + Popularity bias
    - The phenomenon of more popular items being recommended too frequently.
  => Bias in machine learning is unacceptable
  => Bias can be by algorithms and trained data.
  
# Build ethical models
  + Predictive model
    => Adopt PACE framework for creating predictive model.
      - Plan:-
        > What is the purpose of your model?
        > How will its predictions be used?
        > By whom?
        > Who is affected?
        > How harmful/significant could effects be?
      - Analyze :- is your data, 
        > Appropriate?
        > Well sourced?
        > Representative?
      - Construct :-
        > How important is it that your model's predictions be explainable?
      - Execute :-
        > Do your model/predictions make sense?
        > Are predictions fair?
        > Is somebody responsible for reviewing/monitoring model pre- and post-                   deployment?
